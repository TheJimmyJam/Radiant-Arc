✦ Who We Are

Built from the ground up, for the people in it.

Radiant Arc started with a simple belief: that community is the most powerful force for change. We're not a distant institution — we're your neighbors, showing up with food, with open doors, and with open hearts.

Everything we do starts with dignity. We create spaces where people feel seen, valued, and at home — because everyone deserves that foundation.

"I just want it to feel like visiting a state fair booth — warm faces, something good to eat, and the feeling that you belong here."

— Courtney Cannon, Founder

✦ What We Do

Three things we believe in, deeply.

Every program, every gathering, every handshake traces back to these three pillars. They aren't separate — they hold each other up.

🍽️

Pillar One

Full Plates

No one does their best thinking — or their best living — on an empty stomach. We work to make sure good food is never out of reach.

  • Community meals & pop-up pantries
  • Fresh produce access in food deserts
  • Cooking resources & nutrition support
  • No paperwork. No shame. Just food.
🏡

Pillar Two

Safe Places

Stability is the soil everything else grows from. We champion housing, security, and belonging — the right to feel safe where you sleep.

  • Housing navigation & resources
  • Emergency stability support
  • Safe gathering spaces for community
  • Advocacy for housing equity
🌿

Pillar Three

Healing Spaces

Healing isn't a luxury — it's a necessity. We create spaces where people can process, connect, and restore themselves without judgment.

  • Peer support & listening circles
  • Mental health resource navigation
  • Grief, trauma, and recovery support
  • Rest as a radical act

✦ Our Initiatives

Three arms. One heart.

Each initiative is its own thing — its own name, its own vibe — but they all grow from the same roots. Community, dignity, and showing up.

Food Access
🍽️

Full Plate Market

A community market rooted in the belief that access to good food isn't a luxury — it's a foundation. Full Plate Market brings fresh, nourishing food directly to the people who need it most, with zero shame and zero barriers.

Get Involved →

"A full stomach is the first step toward everything else."

  • ◆ Pop-up produce markets in underserved areas
  • ◆ Community meals & shared tables
  • ◆ Pantry partnerships & food drives
  • ◆ No paperwork. No judgment. Just food.
Pet Care
🐾

Scones and Bones

Because pets are family too. Scones and Bones makes sure that financial hardship never means choosing between caring for yourself and caring for your animal. We bring pet care resources to the community with warmth and whimsy.

Get Involved →

"Your pet deserves care just as much as you do."

  • ◆ Pet food & supply giveaways
  • ◆ Vet care navigation & low-cost resources
  • ◆ Foster & rescue support connections
  • ◆ Scones & treats at every pop-up 🐕
Childcare
🌿

The Cabbage Patch

A gentle, grounding space for children and the families who love them. The Cabbage Patch provides childcare support, resources, and connection — because parents deserve community just as much as their kids do.

Get Involved →

"It takes a village. We're building one."

  • ◆ Childcare resources & referrals
  • ◆ Parent support circles & community
  • ◆ Safe, nurturing play & learning spaces
  • ◆ Every child seen, valued, and welcomed
